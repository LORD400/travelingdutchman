Hi everybody! How do you do?

My plan for today is to go to the City Hall, Libraries, Marathon, Little Mermaid & Kastellet Bro.

City Hall & Libraries
Today I looked on Google Maps for entertaining activities. The first was the City Hall, which is a landmark building from 1905. In this building there is also a small old library with nice reading spots and a model of the building. An other room at the entrance, there is the Jens Olsen's World atomic Clock started in the year 1955. The time it takes for the last gear to rotate is 25,753 years, what is insane. It is almost entirely made out of brass and the creator couldn't have seen it, because it was made 10 years after his time. Very interesting time piece and I will be waiting for the whole rotation of the last gear... After this I went to the next library, the black diamond. This is definitely the nicest study space I've been to. They have also a restored part of the old library and this is the study place where minimal/no noise is allowed.
