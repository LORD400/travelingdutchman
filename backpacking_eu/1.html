Hi everybody! How do you do?

And here we go again!!! Today is the first day of many days backpacking through Europe. I'm pretty amazed about it, because it happened so fast from the beginning of planning till today. Hopefully you can enjoy my little stories about the experiences in this journey!

Train to Brussels & lunch
My journey started with a the train from Amsterdam at 11:28 am to the city of Brussels. Maybe one of the best things I've done is first class trains, outlets, big chairs en less crowded. In this time I made some changes for the website where you reading this blog and make it ready for the last pieces of text. After an hour of programming, I looked up some activities for later that day. about 2.5 hours later I arrived and went to the hostel called Youth Hostel Jacques Brel. When in the dorms, I made my bed, put my backpack in the corner and was ready for exploring. At first, it's always kinda tricky to get used to a city, where to go for the main activities. So I started to get some food by a little bakery called Brian Joyeux. It was actually a little pastry shop with a lot of very expansive pastries. They sold also some kind of bread pizza's and my choice was made.

Hi everybody, how do you do?

They offer a basic buffet just to start the day. Afterwards I refreshed myself with a shower and of course a new full body sunscreen protection.

Botanical Garden, Church, exhibition Baroque the Florence
So, now I'm ready and started with a visit to the Botanical Garden. It's a huge park with a big greenhouse for tropical plants. I already did almost the same thing on Copenhagen, where they also have a Botanical Garden. I had a great time and went to an other church near the hostel. Unfortunately it was closed, but the outside was already spectacular. Further in the direction of the grand place, there was an exhibition called Baroque the Florence. There was a fair bit of paintings out of the Renaissance period. Florence was than a huge economical and scientific city, also had a lot of masters of art.
