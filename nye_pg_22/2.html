Exploring the center of lissabon

December 29th 2022 Portugal

Hi everybody! How do you do?

First, we began on foot for a walk through the city, after an excellent buffet. It is an approximately 1.5 hour walk from the hotel to the center, with great differences in altitude. After seeing a palm tree, it truly felt like a holiday and the city is very characteristic. As our departure time was about 10 am, we went to lunch in an Italian restaurant called Republic Italiana. My meal was a Portuguese-inspired calzone.

One of my favorite thing in a new city, is to search for the highest place to have a spectacular overview. Pretty soon we found a fortress 15 minutes from the restaurant, after I've bought some souvenirs of course. When we got there and purchased tickets, our eyes went directly to another building that was much higher than where we were standing... Somewhat disappointed, but it was a lovely view!

Upon the discovery of the fortresses, we had a purpose for tomorrow, the statue of Jesus. Just before the end of the day, we naturally made our way to another restaurant called Dote Cervejaria Moderna. And finished with a few glasses and a few sets of cards.

Till tomorrow!
