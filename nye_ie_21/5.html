The Day After

January 1st 2023 Portugal

Hi everybody! How do you do?

Today was the day, we didn't do much, except going to the buffet. Yesterday was a long night, so we weren't so enthusiastic for a early morning. Before anyone was ready to go out, I took a walk to a nearby park to relax a bit and went on back to the hotel.

Even though, if we would like to take on an activity, almost everything was closed till at least 4 pm. In the afternoon, Marty and I went on a walk looking for sone lunch and we ended by our hero, a little store with only overpriced stuff and candy... It was a healthy lunch...

After around a walk of one and a half hour in search of food it began to heavily rain. Spoiler alert, it was the first and last moment in this vacation!!!

Some time later, we went to an restaurant called Yaso Lamen & Gyoza. Everyone had a different dish of Ramen and it all tasted great! But the quantity was mind bobbling, so much food, we couldn't handle it and had a lot of leftovers... As a dessert there was a bit of muchi and a double espresso (nam-nam-nam).

Till the next one!
